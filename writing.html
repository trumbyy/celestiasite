National Wildlife Federation

This project was also for my Copywriting class.

The objective was to write a radio ad targeting current college students and recent graduates, with a non-monetary call-to-action. My team and I decided to make our focus volunteering.

We strategically chose sound effects that we felt would stick out on the radio, forcing the listener to pause and pay attention.
