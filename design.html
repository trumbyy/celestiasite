Social Media Research Blog

Instaquest

This project was for my Creative Advertising Introductory class.

The objective was to research and report on how brands use Instagram to deliver their advertising and marketing messages. The target audience was advertising and social media marketing practitioners who are employed in professional positions.

I worked on a team of 4 to accomplish this project, utilizing WordPress.com. I held the role of Editor, and found myself taking on duties of Creative Director as well.

One of my duties was to create the logo for our blog.

I wanted the logo to reflect Instagram while still being distinct to our blog. I used a similar font as Instagram’s logo, and took inspiration from the app icon with the “Q.” I placed the logo on a photograph that my group member took of a city skyline, and we used that as our header.

I was also in charge of creating our “Feed” homepage. We wanted this to reflect an Instagram profile.

My group members and I used scholarly journals, trade press, and professional blogs to write weekly articles. Each week is organized into “Quests” and can be found in the menu. Feel free to check out the blog !

Print Magazine Ad

Aim Toothpaste

The objective was to work in a creative team to produce two, full-color, print magazine advertisements for Aim Ultra Mint Gel Toothpaste. The client wanted to reposition the product by framing its “retro-features” as appealing to a new target market segment while retaining its existing market base.

I worked on a team of 6 to accomplish this project. I took the photos, designed the ad and helped write the copy.

The first ad takes on a retro feel, with Polaroids telling the story of a mother passing down her prom dress, as well as her secret to a bright smile.

We chose a tongue-in-cheek approach for our second ad. We played off of the "life hack" trend, and humorously highlighted a well-known toothpaste hack.

I designed the ad, and helped write the copy.
